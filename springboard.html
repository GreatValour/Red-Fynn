Inventory Management

Inventory accuracy in the retail world is very important. You need to be able to easily sell, track, count, and analyze your inventory. Springboard Retail provides you with a complete overview of your inventory management system whether you are a single store or multiple stores. Youll be able to see the status and history of any item, features, and customizability to meet customer demands, and create individual items or bulk items directly from your retail POS system.

Features include:

  • Matrices (3-attribute)
  • Print labels/barcodes both laser and direct thermal
  • Partial and full inventory counts
  • Multiple vendors per item
  • Cost tracking
  • Item stock history
  • Item sale history
  • Customer purchase history
  • Item notes
  • Importing tools
  • Vendor tracking
  • Merge redundant items
  • Multiple SKU fields
  • Archiving
  • Quick editing
  • Conditional batch editing
  • Tracks shipping
  • Auto-fill searches
  • Multi-store inventory tracking
  • Re-order points/levels

  • Drop shipments
