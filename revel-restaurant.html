Cash Management

In a mostly digital society, there are still people who use cash, which is why you need a cash management solution for your restaurant. Revel POS will take control of your cash flow with till management. To monitor the cash coming in, we have rich permissions settings and a log that tracks activity, set alerts, and prompts you when the till reaches a certain amount. Another great feature is you can tie tills to individual employees rather than a cash box to accurately note the end of day totals to the right employees.
