Retailcloud POS

Intuitive and simple to use design makes mastering the TabPOS easy for cashiers and managers alike.

Fast, Intuitive, and Familiar

Easy to use layout allows most transactions to be completed in two or three clicks.

Adding Items to the Cart

Create Quick Pick Pages to easily click on items or scan barcodes to ring up items. An Open Ring button is always available.

Refunds, Sales & Exchanges

Process Sales, Refunds and Exchanges from the same screen. No need to do Exchanges in two transactions.

Discounts & Coupons

Easily handle Transaction and Item Level Promotion, Discounts and Coupons with detailed back office reporting.

Clienteling Mode

Switch to clienteling mode to allow your sales associates on the floor to improve customer engagement.

Product

View styles by classification or attribute to find what exactly what your customer needs and show them with screen images.

Styles and Colors

Once your customer finds a product they like, get color, size and product descriptions with a click of a button.

Customer Preferences

Look up a customer and get access to all their preferences and history, so associates can better meet the customer needs.

Associate Reporting

Attach transactions and items to one or a combination of associates, so you can run productivity and commission reports.

Insightful Reporting

At a glance get an overview of how your business is doing with clear-cut graphs and reports.

Total Sales and Units

Get readings on your total sales and the number of units moved. Units per transaction are a key indicator of how effective your product mix is displayed.

CE Index

CE Index is an algorithm that rates your customer engagement. The higher the number, the more effective your team is performing.

Staffing Hours and More

Quickly see the total hours worked, cross reference it to margin reports on the ngauge dashboard to see how you are doing. Many printed reports are also available on the POS and on the portal.

Extend your Point of Sale

Your management portal provides real time visibility to all your POS and ecommerce activity.
See your sales and inventory status at any time. Changes made at the portal are updated to all your devices.

Connect

Using your secure cloud account, all your POS devices connect with each other. Anything you do on one is available on all your devices.

Dynamic

Get automatic updates and upgrades, as features get added to keep you responsive to industry trends and customer requirements.

Support

Best in Class support available from our San Francisco based support team, who are knowledgeable on our software and retail operations.

Popular Modules

TabPOS can support several different plugins and modules. Here are three of the most popular ones.

Loyalty

Create your own loyalty reward program using visits, items or amount spent to earn points for rebates or merchandise.

Skedool

Keep track of your appointments and reservations easily with reminders and even waitlist capabilities.

Time & Attendance

Have employees clock in and out on the POS, and see all activity on the portal. Create files to export into your payroll service.
